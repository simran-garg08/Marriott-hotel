The Marriott Palace, Mumbai
A stunning heritage palace that overlooks the jewel of Mumbai's skyline - Gateway of India, offers a remarkable blend of traditional elegance and modern sophistication. It is the flagship hotel of Marriott Hotels,Resort & Palaces and is country’s first harbour landmark. The golden-themed Ballroom with theatre style seating can host up to 300 guests while for intimate celebrations you can opt for the rooftop space for up to 80 people.

Marriott Palace, Delhi
A regal wonder, hidden right in plain sight, amidst boundless greenery and away from the bustling streets of New Delhi - Taj Palace, Delhi, is an ideal destination to create and cherish beautiful wedding moments with your loved ones. Exchange promises of a lifetime as the skyline of New Delhi unfolds before you, playing witness to your divine union.

Marriott Bengal
Nestled in the greenest corner of Kolkata, the Taj Bengal's outstanding views of the sprawling cityscape and the Alipore Zoological Gardens make it one of the dreamiest places for a city wedding! Lush green banquet lawns hold the capacity of 400 guests while the fine Crystal Room with exquisite interiors extend it to 700 – Providing a wide variety for celebration selections. Art, culture, heritage, and luxury come together in one single masterpiece to bring you picturesque moments you will cherish for eternity.
